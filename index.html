About Us

SharpSquare was founded by three UC Berkeley grads and avid sports fans. While watching games with our friends, we often found ourselves making predictions throughout the game — anything from ‘LeBron is about to score at least 15 points this quarter’ to ‘I bet the Bucks next basket will be a three pointer.’ Small side bets and bragging rights ensued.

Eventually, we began to ask: Why should this experience be limited to a few friends sitting around in their living room? So, we created SharpSquare, a way for fans to test out their knowledge, compete for bragging rights and prizes, and get even more engaged with the game. We’ll be launching our mobile app shortly, and in the meantime we’ll be hosting a limited number of contests centered around NBA playoff games, starting with Game 3 in the Rockets Warriors Series.
