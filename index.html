Why SharpSquare?

Credibility

Existing apps allow you to submit picks on games that have already occured. We only allow you to submit picks before the action - so, when you see another user's winning percentage, you know that it's the real deal.

About Us

SharpSquare was founded by two UC Berkeley grads and avid sports bettors. Both of us often use platforms like Reddit, Covers, and the Action Network to connect with other sports bettors, share our opinions, and get insight from others. However, at one point we began to ask ourselves: How do we know who to trust? How do we know if we’re getting tips from a skilled professional or a clueless amateur?

To fill this credibility gap, we created SharpSquare, a way to 1) show others that you’re legit and 2) check if others can back up their talk. This free tool is intended for seasoned vets looking for more legitimacy, amateurs looking for trustworthy advice, and everyone in between. Sign up for our waitlist today.
