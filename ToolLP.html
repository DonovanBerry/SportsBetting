Why SharpSquare?

Connect

If you've seen a sportsbook on an NFL Sunday, you know that sports betting brings people together. Our iOS app brings this experience to your phone.

About Us

SharpSquare was founded by two UC Berkeley grads and avid sports bettors. Back in college, we used to love taking group trips to Vegas during football season — we’d make the eight hour drive on Friday, hang out in a sportsbook on Saturday and Sunday, and then make it home by Monday for classes.

After graduating college, we quickly realized that we didn't have the time for our regular Vegas excursions. However, we longed for the thrill of those leather recliners at the Bellagio on an NFL Sunday: making friends, high fiving complete strangers with money on the same teams ... and drowning our sorrows at the bar after a bad day. So, we created SharpSquare, a place for gamblers to be before, during, and after games. While nothing can match the excitement of a Vegas sportsbook, we’re giving you the next best thing, all at the touch of your fingertips. Sign up today to join our waitlist.
